Ravioli

Description

Ravioli is a delightful Italian pasta dish filled with various ingredients, typically cheese or meat.
This recipe will guide you through the steps to prepare a delicious plate of ravioli that you and your family will savor.

Ingredients

  • 1 package (16 ounces) of fresh or frozen ravioli
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook the ravioli according to the package instructions until they float to the surface. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, melt the unsalted butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a gentle simmer. Cook for a few minutes until the cream thickens slightly.
  5. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it's well combined and the sauce is creamy. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  6. Toss the cooked ravioli into the skillet, coating them with the creamy sauce.
  7. Serve the ravioli hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and additional Parmesan cheese if desired.
