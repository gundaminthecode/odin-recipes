Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a classic and easy-to-make Italian pasta dish. This recipe will guide you through the steps to prepare a delicious plate of spaghetti that you and your family will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for a minute or until fragrant.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes and red pepper flakes (if using). Season with salt and black pepper. Simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Toss the cooked spaghetti into the skillet and mix with the sauce until well coated.
  6. Remove from heat and stir in the chopped basil.
  7. Serve the spaghetti hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.
